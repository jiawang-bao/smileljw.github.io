Biography

I finished my undergraduate at Harbin Engineering University (HEU), where I learned how to become an engineer. I am a master student who graduated from Southeast University in 2022, where I learned how to become a researcher. I am currently working as a system engineer in Huawei Co. Ltd, working in the field of millimeter wave radar applications.

Influenced by my father since I was a child, I am very interested in science and have grown up in practice.When I was in college, I read a lot of books about science and technology to increase my knowledge and broaden my horizons.In my spare time, I participated in many electronic design competitions and got good results.

2-D Orthogonal Multibeam Antenna Arrays for 5G Millimeter-Wave Applications
In this article, a 2-D 16-beam MBAA with 16 × 16 SIW Butler matrix and a 2-D APHMBAA with an orthogonal beamforming architecture are proposed, designed, and demon strated. Their properties indicate that the two 2-D MBAA could both provide the most promising candidates for the next-generation mobile communication system.
Weiyang Kong, Yun Hu, Jiawang Li, Lei Zhang and Wei Hong

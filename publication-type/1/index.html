Design and Implementation of mmWave Phased-array Radar for Three-dimensional Imaging
A mmWave dual polarized antenna with filtering response and low cross polarization for 5G mobile communication is proposed. It not only avoids the use of additional filters which will bring some insertion loss and increase circuit board area, but also achieves very low cross polarization characteristic. In addition, the antenna has a simple structure which makes manufacturing easier. The proposed antenna and array can be a competitive candidate for 5G practical applications.
Jiawang Li, Wei Hong, Yun Hu, Huimin Liu, Gang Xu, Yuanwei Zhu, Zongyu Zhang and Zhenyu Pan

Millimeter Wave Filtenna with Compact Size for 5G Applications
In this paper, a filtenna with only 4-layers metals for 5G mmWave application is proposed. A simple but effective way of feeding and radiation makes the design simple and increases the practical value of the antenna. The filtenna is a good candidate for mmWave 5G applications.
Jiawang Li, Yun Hu, Lei Xiang, Guangwen Jin, Zhenyu Pan and Hong Wei

Millimeter Wave Broadband Circularly Polarized Antenna and Array with Stacked Structure
A mmWave broadband CP stacked antenna is proposed. The CP bandwidth of the antenna can be broadened by utilizing 4 driven patches and one radiation patch. The antenna provides a promising solution for mmWave 5G application.
Jiawang Li, Yun Hu, Lei Xiang, Zhi Hui Ma and Hong Wei
